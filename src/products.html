Artisan is a clothing line made locally in Benguet. The style of the apparel is based on the local designs which are inspired by the culture and art styles of Benguet. It emphasizes the designs that symbolizes the styles, design, and culture of cordillerans. Even the fabric that is used for making the Artisan apparel are made within Benguet. The apparel line aims to promote and introduce the art that portrays the identity of the province.
